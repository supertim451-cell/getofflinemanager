About

OfflineManager was born out of a simple but powerful idea: your passwords should belong to you, and only you. In an age where everything is moving to the cloud, we wanted to create something different — a password manager that works entirely offline, ensuring that your most sensitive information never leaves your device. What started as a small project between friends, driven by the frustration of data breaches and unwanted tracking, has now grown into a tool trusted by privacy-conscious users worldwide. We believe in complete transparency, strong encryption, and giving you full control over your digital identity. OfflineManager is built to be simple, secure, and reliable — whether you’re storing a handful of passwords or managing hundreds. This is not just software, it’s a promise: your data, your device, your control.
